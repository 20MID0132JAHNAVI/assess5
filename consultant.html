Job Description

and the customer.You will also be responsible for providing information on the product and service to the
clients.In addition to this, you should maintain and manage a database of client's information. You should be
able to resolve all the issues and complaints. Prior work experience as a Customer Service Consultant will
be an added benefit to the candidates.
